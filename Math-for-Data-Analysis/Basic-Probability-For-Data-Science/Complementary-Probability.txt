# Complementary Probability – Step-by-Step Solutions

## 🔹 Question 1

**Probability of not getting heads (i.e., tails):**

P = 1 - 1/2 = 1/2

✅ **Answer: 1/2**

---

## 🔹 Question 2

**Probability of not getting a 4:**

P = 1 - 1/6 = 5/6

✅ **Answer: 5/6**

---

## 🔹 Question 3

Total kings = 4 in 52 cards

P(not king) = 1 - 4/52 = 48/52 = 12/13

✅ **Answer: 12/13**

## 🔹 Question 4

P(fail) = 1 - 0.7 = 0.3

## 🔹 Question 5

We use complement:

Probability of **at least one head** = 1 − probability of **no heads**

Probability of no heads (all tails in 3 tosses):

(1/2)^3 = 1/8

So,

P(at least one head) = 1 - 1/8 = 7/8

✅ **Answer: 7/8**
